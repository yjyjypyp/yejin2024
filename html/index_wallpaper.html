Vol. 1   /   March 2024


The wallpaper project is a regular publication initiative for this website, featuring images created based on the Ye Jin Choi's diaries and snapshots from daily life. It draws inspiration from diary entries and photos dating back to 2022, capturing the artist's reflections on multiple occurrences of the same season experienced in London.
Throughout the project, similarities in emotions felt during the same period are discovered, along with glimpses of new daily life experiences on specific days.

The keywords for the March 2024 edition are 'weather,' 'spring,' and 'vibrancy'. March in London brings a sense of impending spring, yet the weather remains cloudy with frequent rain showers. This month's images portray the anticipation of spring that seems to be on its way but has yet to arrive, reflecting the many days spent waiting for its arrival.
