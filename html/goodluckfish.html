Acrylic Keychain
2025
The pollock fish is said to symbolize “a fish that brings brightness.” In traditional Korean culture, it was believed that hanging a pollock in the home would ward off misfortune and invite good luck.

Taken from Revé’s writings, the title phrase offers a fascinating reflection on the nature of belief. At times, we find comfort in symbols and stories, regardless of their truth, and they provide us with a sense of solace. These small objects may be more than just decorative items—they could embody the belief or energy you wish to carry with you. What will you choose to believe in this year?
